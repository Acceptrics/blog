Pro Plan, Cookie Scanner & a Redesigned Account — Spring 2026 Update

This has been the biggest release cycle for Acceptrics since launch. We shipped a paid Pro tier, a fully automated Cookie Scanner add-on, multilingual support for 26 languages, a visual Theme Picker, and a completely redesigned Account page. Here's everything that's new.

Introducing the Pro Plan

Acceptrics now has a Pro plan for teams that need more than the free tier. Pro unlocks premium add-ons (starting with the Cookie Scanner), and is billed monthly at a flat rate. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime straight from your Account page — no support ticket needed. Payment is handled securely via Stripe.

Cookie Scanner — Automatic Compliance Auditing

Pro subscribers can now enable the Cookie Scanner, a new add-on that automatically crawls your site and inventories every cookie it sets. The scanner checks your banner configuration against what it finds and surfaces any cookies that aren't yet covered.

To turn it on, open your Account page, go to Add-ons, and toggle on Cookie Scanner. You can target specific domains or let it cover everything on your account. Scans run on a recurring schedule — no manual steps required.

Banner Theme Picker

Your banner now has a Theme Picker built into the wizard. Choose from a set of curated color schemes — Light, Dark, Ocean, Slate, and more — or keep your existing custom styling. Theme changes preview live before you save.

26-Language Support

The consent banner now ships translations for 26 languages, including all major European languages, Japanese, Korean, and Brazilian Portuguese. Language is detected automatically from the visitor's browser locale — nothing to configure.

Redesigned Account Page

The Account page has been rebuilt from the ground up. Your subscription status, active add-ons, domain-level usage stats, and banner health issues are all visible at a glance in a clean grid layout. If any of your banners have configuration problems, a health alert appears inline with a direct link to fix it.

Log in to your account to explore everything that's new.

Enhanced Conversions Now Available!

We are excited to announce that Acceptrics now supports Enhanced Conversions—a powerful feature designed to improve your marketing attribution and overall campaign performance.

How It Works

With the enhanced conversions update, simply select the "Enhanced Conversion" radio button in the Acceptrics wizard during setup. Once enabled, our system automatically detects personally identifiable information (PII) on your webpages where Acceptrics is installed. This PII is then used to enable better attribution of conversions without any additional manual configuration.

Note: Double-check if sending visitor PII is right for your business needs before enabling.

Why Enhanced Conversions?

Enhanced conversions allow you to measure the full impact of your consent banner installation by seamlessly integrating conversion data with your analytics platforms. This means:

  • Improved campaign performance measurement
  • Better customization of your user experiences
  • Accurate and enhanced conversion attribution with minimal setup effort

Start using Enhanced Conversions today to unlock the true potential of your website's data. To get started, simply head over to the Acceptrics wizard and enable the feature.
