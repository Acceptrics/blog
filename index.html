Enhanced Conversions Now Available!

We are excited to announce that Acceptrics now supports Enhanced Conversions—a powerful feature designed to improve your marketing attribution and overall campaign performance.

How It Works

With the enhanced conversions update, simply select the "Enhanced Conversion" radio button in the Acceptrics wizard during setup. Once enabled, our system automatically detects personally identifiable information (PII) on your webpages where Acceptrics is installed. This PII is then used to enable better attribution of conversions without any additional manual configuration.

Note: Double-check if sending visitor PII is right for your business needs before enabling.

Why Enhanced Conversions?

Enhanced conversions allow you to measure the full impact of your consent banner installation by seamlessly integrating conversion data with your analytics platforms. This means:

  • Improved campaign performance measurement
  • Better customization of your user experiences
  • Accurate and enhanced conversion attribution with minimal setup effort

Start using Enhanced Conversions today to unlock the true potential of your website's data. To get started, simply head over to the Acceptrics wizard and enable the feature.
